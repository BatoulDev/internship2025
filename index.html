Blazer Style Guide

Explore classic & modern blazers for every occasion

What is a Blazer?

A blazer is a timeless jacket that can be dressed up or down. It adds sophistication to both casual and formal outfits, making it an essential wardrobe piece.

Popular Types of Blazers

  • Single-Breasted Blazer
  • Double-Breasted Blazer
  • Cotton Casual Blazer
  • Velvet Blazer for Evenings

Styling Tips

Match your blazer with chinos or jeans for a laid-back look, or wear it with tailored trousers for a formal event. Add accessories like a pocket square or wristwatch to complete the outfit.
